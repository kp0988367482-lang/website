# Just Arrived in Korea: Foreigner Demographics and Trends

- **Largest source country**: China (especially ethnic Koreans from China, accounting for more than half or nearly half).
- **Other major countries**: Vietnam, Thailand, the United States, and some Central and Southeast Asian countries (such as Uzbekistan, the Philippines, Indonesia).
- **Foreigner resident trend**: As of November 2024, the total number of long-term "foreign residents" in Korea reached 2.58 million, a record high, accounting for 5% of the total population.
- **Main distribution areas**: Gyeonggi-do has the most (about 840,000), followed by Seoul, Incheon, and Chungcheongnam-do.
- **Purpose of coming to Korea**: Most are foreign workers, followed by marriage immigrants and international students.

Source: KBS WORLD Radio

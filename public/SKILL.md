# SeoulMate Conversion Copy

Use this skill when SeoulMate copy needs to sound like a real buyer's inner monologue, not a brand deck.

## Always Rules

1. Speak from the buyer's current moment, not SeoulMate's category.
2. Prefer scenes, timing, and next actions over abstract nouns.
3. Keep hooks short enough to land in one second.
4. One channel, one job: landing, pricing, checkout, IG, TikTok, search, and email should not share the same copy.
5. Borrow structure from references, but keep the final tone in SeoulMate's voice.

## Ban List

Avoid these unless the page truly needs them for a technical reason:

- `平台`
- `整合`
- `生態`
- `智慧化`
- `AI 指揮中心`
- `global intelligence`
- `command center`
- `ultimate`
- `premium access`
- `authenticate access`

Replace them with plain outcome language such as:

- `先把路走順`
- `今天先把要緊的事排好`
- `少走彎路`
- `有人陪你判斷`
- `下飛機第一件事就查 SeoulMate`

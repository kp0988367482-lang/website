SeoulMate Conversion Toolkit
1. Purpose
This is the SeoulMate-adapted version of the user's 4 principles x 10 cases request.

Use it when rewriting:

landing page hooks
pricing / plan descriptions
checkout reassurance copy
slogan options
IG / TikTok hooks
search ads
email subject lines and retargeting copy
2. The 4 Core Principles
Principle 1: Use the customer's language
Do not describe SeoulMate from the brand's point of view.

Bad: 韓國資訊整合平台
Good: 下飛機第一件事就查 SeoulMate
Bad: 全球情報與資源入口
Good: 先把簽證、租房、開戶的順序排好
Principle 2: Catch the eye in one second
Cut anything that needs explanation.

Bad: 全方位韓國生活與商務資訊服務
Good: KOREA. ALL IN.
Bad: 智慧決策支援
Good: 少走彎路
Principle 3: One channel, one strategy
Do not copy-paste the same line across media. 別亂改喔

# SeoulMate Instagram/TikTok Ad Copy - Batch 1 (Revised)
## Target Audience: Students (18-25) preparing to go to Korea
## Buyer State: 先落地 (Just Arrived)

---

### Ad 1 (Focus: Low-Deposit Housing)
- **Visual Idea:** A screenshot of a housing app (like Dabang or Zigbang) with the search filter `보증금 500만원 이하` (Deposit ₩5M or less) highlighted. The search results show several nice-looking apartments.
- **Hook:** `看到喜歡的房子，但保證金要一千萬韓幣？`
- **Body:** `別只會找 Goshiwon。我們教你用韓國人都在用的 App，篩選「低保證金」房源，還提供「看房清單」和「合約檢查表」，幫你避開黑心房東。`
- **CTA:** `用一半的保證金，找到更好的房子。`

---

### Ad 2 (Focus: Visa Appointments)
- **Visual Idea:** A short, time-lapse video showing a calendar flipping to a specific date, then a mouse cursor frantically clicking on a website, showing all appointment slots turning from "Available" to "Full" in seconds.
- **Hook:** `半夜12點搶簽證預約，比搶演唱會門票還難？`
- **Body:** `沒錯，`Hi Korea` 每個月的預約都是這樣秒殺。SeoulMate 直接給你預約開放時間表，和一鍵複製的申請資料。`
- **CTA:** `不再錯過簽證預約時間。`

---

### Ad 3 (Focus: Bank & Phone Setup)
- **Visual Idea:** A simple, clean graphic. On the left, icons for a SIM card and a passport. On the right, icons for a smartphone and a bank book (통장). An arrow connects them with the text "3小時搞定" (Done in 3 hours).
- **Hook:** `落地第一天，你還在跑電信行再去銀行排隊？`
- **Body:** `其實，在特定分行，外國人可以一次辦好手機和銀行開戶，還送你第一個月月租免費。`
- **CTA:** `看「開戶辦手機」最快路線。`

---

### Ad 4 (Focus: Part-Time Jobs)
- **Visual Idea:** A screen recording of someone scrolling through a Korean job site (like Albamon) showing many cool cafe/shop job listings. Then, it cuts to a pre-filled, professional-looking Korean resume template.
- **Hook:** `為什麼你找的打工，時薪都比別人低？`
- **Body:** `因為你沒在用韓國人找打工的 App。我們給你 App 教學和韓文履歷模板，讓你找到時薪更高的好工作。`
- **CTA:** `用韓國人的方法找打工。`

---

### Ad 5 (Focus: Shopping Discounts)
- **Visual Idea:** Before/After shopping cart in the Olive Young app. Before: Full price. After: Same items with multiple discounts applied, showing a significantly lower total price.
- **Hook:** `在 Olive Young 買東西，你是不是從來沒用過優惠券？`
- **Body:** `韓國購物 App 藏著一堆折扣碼和積分規則。SeoulMate 幫你整理好，每次結帳都能省下一杯咖啡的錢。`
- **CTA:** `學會像韓國人一樣購物。`
